• How to log in to Gmail

    1. Go to gmail.com. If you're already a Gmail user, you might be logged in already to your personal account. To add an account:
      1. Click on the circular profile image at the top right and select Add another account.
    2. Enter your school provided email and password.
      1. This was emailed to you from help@astravo.org - titled "Welcome! Here are your login credentials..."
    3. If this is your first time logging in...Change your password! The default password you were given is not a secure password. Please follow these steps to change it as soon as possible!
      1. Click on the G Suite box with the circular profile image at the top right, and then click Manage your Google Account
      2. Click Security in the left-hand menu
      3. Click Password
      4. Follow the prompts - entering your old password and new password
        1. Make sure to make your password something you can remember!
    4. In your school portal, use the Sign in with Google button!

  • Tips for navigating courses

    1. Go to your school portal
    2. From your Dashboard, click on the name of a course in the Academic Snapshotbox.
    3. This takes you to the Student App page where you can access your individual courses.
    4. Click on a course card to "go to" that class
    5. In the course you'll find ...
      1. A Resources folder
        1. Here, you'll find the Materials and supplies that you'll need for that class.
        2. As well as other helpful information like How To Videos
      2. A Teacher Information and Links folder
        1. In the Teacher Information and Links folder, you'll find Teacher Contact Information so that you can reach your teacher.
        2. You'll also find Synchronous Support options.
          1. This is where you'll find when your live classes are scheduled, how to find the recordings if you miss the live class, and how to sign up for one-on-one time with your teacher during their office hours.
    6. At the top of each course you'll find a To Do List.
      1. This shows you what upcoming assignments you have due and will help you stay on pace.
    7. To access your Calendar, click on the three horizontal lines on the top left of your screen.
      1. Next, click on the Calendar icon.
      2. You will see a day-by-day view of what you should be doing in all of your classes.
      3. A check mark will appear next to assignments that you have already completed, so feel free to work ahead.
    8. Clicking on an assignment in the Calendar will take you directly to the activity.
      1. You can move forward and backward between activities by clicking on Next Activity and Previous Activity at the bottom of your screen.
    9. You might want to go back and review an activity before taking a quiz.
      1. When an activity is complete, that enables you to take a quiz.
      2. If you try to skip an activity, you will see a message that says that you must successfully complete the activities before viewing the item.
    10. Hitting the back arrow at the top left will take you back to where you previously were.
    11. Using the three horizontal lines:
      1. Click on Student App...
      2. ...to take you back to your Course Cards.
    12. We’re so glad that you’ve chosen to school with us! You can contact us by emailing help@astravo.org with questions.
